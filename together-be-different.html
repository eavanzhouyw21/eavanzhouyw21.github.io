Together Be Different

A collaborative tabletop RPG that celebrates diversity and emphasizes teamwork through unique character abilities and inclusive storytelling

RPG Collaborative Storytelling Inclusive

Project Overview

Together Be Different is a tabletop RPG designed to celebrate diversity and promote inclusive storytelling. The game focuses on collaborative problem-solving where each character's unique abilities and perspectives are essential for success.

Unlike traditional RPGs that often emphasize conflict and competition, Together Be Different encourages players to work together, combining their different strengths to overcome challenges. The system rewards creative solutions and collaborative thinking while providing a framework for meaningful character development.

Core Mechanics

Unique Abilities

Each character has distinct abilities that complement others, making every player essential to the team's success.

Collaborative Resolution

Challenges require multiple characters working together, combining their different approaches and perspectives.

Inclusive Character Creation

Character creation system celebrates diverse backgrounds, abilities, and identities as sources of strength.

Narrative Focus

Mechanics support storytelling and character development over combat and competition.

Development Journey

The game evolved through multiple prototypes based on extensive playtesting:

Prototype 1: Foundation

Initial concept focusing on collaborative mechanics and character diversity. Established core philosophy and basic resolution system.

Playtest & Refinement

First playtest revealed needs for clearer GM guidance and more structured collaborative mechanics.

Prototype 2: Streamlining

Simplified mechanics while maintaining collaborative focus. Improved character sheet design and reference materials.

Advanced Playtesting

Extensive testing with diverse groups validated the inclusive design and collaborative mechanics.

Prototype 3: Final Build

Polished system with comprehensive GM and player guides, balanced mechanics, and clear presentation.

Design Philosophy

Together Be Different was built on several core principles:

Celebration of Difference

Differences in ability, background, and perspective are portrayed as strengths that enhance group success.

Collaborative Problem-Solving

Challenges require multiple approaches, encouraging players to think creatively and work together.

Inclusive Representation

Character options and scenarios represent diverse identities and experiences authentically.

Positive Gameplay

Mechanics focus on building up rather than tearing down, creating positive gaming experiences.

Key Features

  • Collaborative mechanics that require teamwork
  • Inclusive character creation celebrating diversity
  • Streamlined resolution system focused on storytelling
  • Comprehensive GM guidance for inclusive storytelling
  • Multiple campaign frameworks and scenarios
  • Professional layout and accessible design
  • Extensive playtesting validation
  • Available on itch.io for community access

Project Details

Timeline: 12 months
Players: 3-6 players + GM
Session Length: 2-4 hours
Age Range: 12+
Prototypes: 3 major iterations
