About Me

Passionate about creating meaningful gaming experiences

Hi, I'm Eavan

I'm a game designer with a passion for creating engaging experiences that bring people together. My work spans both analog and digital mediums, from strategic card games and collaborative RPGs to innovative video games.

What drives me is the power of games to create meaningful connections between players. Whether it's the strategic depth of a well-designed card game, the collaborative storytelling of an RPG, or the atmospheric immersion of a digital experience, I believe games have the unique ability to foster engagement, creativity, and social interaction.

Design Philosophy

My approach to game design is rooted in iterative development and player feedback. I believe in:

  • Player-Centered Design: Every mechanic serves the player experience
  • Iterative Development: Constant playtesting and refinement
  • Meaningful Choices: Decisions that matter and create engagement
  • Inclusive Design: Games that welcome diverse players and perspectives
