Mad Surgeons

A darkly humorous strategic card game where players compete to build the most impressive creature from body parts

Card Game Strategy Dark Humor Competitive

Project Overview

Mad Surgeons is a strategic card game that combines dark humor with engaging mechanics. Players take on the role of eccentric surgeons competing to create the most impressive creature by collecting and assembling various body parts.

The game features a unique assembly system where players must strategically collect different types of body parts (heads, torsos, limbs) while managing their hand and competing for limited resources. The final creature's "impressiveness" is determined by various scoring mechanisms that reward both completion and creativity.

Game Mechanics

Body Part Collection

Collect heads, torsos, arms, and legs to build your creature. Each part type has unique properties and scoring values.

Assembly System

Connect body parts following anatomical rules while maximizing scoring combinations and special abilities.

Heart Cards

Special heart cards are required to bring your creature to life, adding a crucial timing element to the game.

Competitive Drafting

Players compete for the best parts while managing limited hand space and timing their moves.

Design Process

Mad Surgeons went through extensive design and refinement:

Card Design System

Developed a comprehensive card layout system using Adobe InDesign with data merge functionality for efficient production of 100+ unique cards.

Visual Identity

Created distinctive artwork for each body part type with consistent color coding and iconography to aid gameplay clarity.

Rulebook Development

Comprehensive rulebook with illustrated examples, edge case clarifications, and strategic tips for players.

Print-Ready Production

Prepared professional print files with proper bleeds, color management, and cut guides for manufacturing.

Key Features

  • 120+ unique body part cards with distinct abilities
  • Strategic hand management and timing decisions
  • Multiple scoring paths for different strategies
  • Dark humor theme with engaging artwork
  • Scalable gameplay for 2-6 players
  • Professional card design and production files
  • Comprehensive rulebook with examples

Component Design

The game features professionally designed components:

  • Playing Cards: 100+ unique body part cards with custom artwork
  • Special Cards: Heart cards and action cards with distinct designs
  • Card Backs: Unified design system for easy identification
  • Reference Materials: Quick-reference cards and player aids

Project Details

Players: 2-6 players
Play Time: 30-45 minutes
Components: 120+ cards, rulebook
