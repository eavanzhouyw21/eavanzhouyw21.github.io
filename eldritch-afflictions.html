Eldritch Afflictions

A horror-themed puzzle video game combining surgical mechanics with atmospheric design to create an unsettling and engaging experience

Video Game Horror Puzzle Atmospheric

Project Overview

Eldritch Afflictions is a horror-themed puzzle game that places players in the role of a surgeon dealing with otherworldly infections and parasites. The game combines precise surgical mechanics with atmospheric horror elements to create a unique and unsettling gameplay experience.

Players must carefully extract various parasites and afflictions from infected body parts using surgical tools, all while managing limited resources and dealing with increasingly disturbing cases. The game's hand-drawn art style and surgical precision mechanics create tension between careful gameplay and horror atmosphere.

Core Gameplay

Surgical Precision

Use various surgical tools (scalpels, forceps, clamps) to carefully extract parasites without damaging healthy tissue.

Puzzle Mechanics

Each case presents unique challenges requiring different approaches and tool combinations to solve successfully.

Progressive Difficulty

Cases become more complex with multiple parasites, unusual anatomy, and time pressure elements.

Atmospheric Horror

Unsettling visuals and sound design create tension while maintaining focus on precise gameplay.

Development Process

The game was developed through a comprehensive design thinking process:

Concept Development

Initial brainstorming explored the intersection of medical procedures and horror elements, leading to the surgical puzzle concept.

Critical Thinking Process

Documented analysis of horror game mechanics, puzzle design principles, and user interface considerations for precision-based gameplay.

Prototype Development

Created hand-drawn prototype to test core mechanics before digital implementation, validating the surgical precision concept.

Art Asset Creation

Developed distinctive hand-drawn sprites for body parts, parasites, and surgical tools with consistent atmospheric style.

Implementation & Polish

Digital implementation with refined mechanics, user interface, and atmospheric elements integrated seamlessly.

Art & Design

The visual design of Eldritch Afflictions emphasizes atmosphere and precision:

Hand-Drawn Sprites

All game assets were created as hand-drawn illustrations, giving the game a unique artistic identity and unsettling atmosphere.

Surgical Tools

Detailed representations of medical instruments (forceps, scalpels, clamps) with intuitive visual feedback for player actions.

Anatomical Accuracy

Body parts and procedures maintain medical accuracy while incorporating fantastical horror elements.

Parasite Design

Unique creature designs for each type of affliction, ranging from worms and neurons to more abstract eldritch entities.

Technical Features

  • Precision-based input system for surgical accuracy
  • Multiple body part puzzles (hands, feet, abdomen)
  • Variety of surgical tools with unique mechanics
  • Progressive difficulty with increasingly complex cases
  • Hand-drawn art assets with atmospheric consistency
  • Intuitive user interface for medical procedures
  • Physical component integration for tactile feedback
  • Available on itch.io for community access
